<b>Definition.</b><br/>GridValuesMatrix is a subclass of Grid that ties feature PropertyValues to grid geometry. It has three attributes: Values, SequencingRule and StartSequence. It holds a sequence of records associated with a sequencing rule that specifies an algorithm for assigning records of PropertyValues to grid points. <br/>
