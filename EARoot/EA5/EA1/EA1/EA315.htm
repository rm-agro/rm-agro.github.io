<b>Definition.</b><br/>A grid is a network composed of two or more sets of curves in which the members of each set intersect the members of the other sets on a systematic way. <br/><br/><b>Remark.</b><br/>The curves are called grid lines, the points at which they intersect are grid points and the interstices between the grid lines are grid cells.<br/>
