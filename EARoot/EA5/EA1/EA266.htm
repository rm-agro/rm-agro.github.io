<b>Description.</b><br/>the <b>PropertyValue</b> describes a single value of a single <b>PropertyVariable</b> which is part of a <b>DataSet</b> and valid for the object (<b>PropertyZone</b>, <b>TreatmentZone</b>, <b>VerticalLayer</b> of the soil or in the canopy, <b>Batch</b>, <b>Sample</b>, etc.) as a whole, or, in case of an object that has spatial characteristics, for a specific geography within the object.<br/>
