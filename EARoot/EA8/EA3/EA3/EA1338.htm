-- Name --<br/>AbstractInstallation<br/>-- Definition --<br/>Stationary technical unit part of a facility where one or more Agricultural and Aquacultural activities are carried out, and any other directly associated activities which have a technical connection with the activities carried out on that site.<br/>-- Description -- <br/>Agricultural and Aquacultural Activities refers to the production of primary materials (animal and vegetal) for human use, or as base material for industrial processing (animal feed, human food or other industries).<br/>Depending on the scale, the installations would be represented by areas or points and always linked with a Facility (at less in a 1:1 relation).<br/><br/>
