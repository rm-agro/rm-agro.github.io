<b>Definition.</b><br/>Equipment is a technical assembly of components to realize certain activities. Equipment is treated as one economic unit.<br/><br/><b>Remark.</b><br/>Equipment is a super class for <b>Tractor</b>'s, <b>ImplementAssembly</b>'s and <b>SensorSystem</b>'s.<br/><br/>An Equipment can exist of one or more implements. In case of more they are assembled in such a way that they are treated as one unit.<br/>
