<b>Description.</b><br/>A Farm is part of an AgriculturalProductionOrganization located at a distinct geographic location with the objective to produce crops. <br/><br/><b>Remark.</b><br/>An AgriculturalProductionOrganization can have one or more Farms. <br/><br/>A farm is assumed to be operated as an independent unit and is as such also an Organization.<br/><br/>An AgriculturalProductionOrganization can have also other agricultural production activities like greenhouse crop production or an animal holding.<br/>
