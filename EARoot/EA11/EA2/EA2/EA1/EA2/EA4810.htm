<b>Definition</b><br/>An <b>ActivityField</b> is a continuous surface of land on which activities are performed; i.e. <b>Task</b>s and <b>Operation</b>s. <br/><br/><b>Remarks.</b><br/>The <b>ActivityField</b> can be identical to a <b>Field</b> or is part of a <b>Field. </b>In many cases it will correspond with a <b>CropField</b>, but this is not always the case; <br/>1) Activities can be performed on a<b> Field</b> as a whole, when there are no crops grown, <br/>2) Activities can be performed on more <b>CropField</b>s bordering each other, when there is a reason to distinguish them as <b>CropField</b>s, but operations can be executed as one activity and <br/>3) Activities are performed on only a part of the <b>CropField</b>, like for example when a contractor is asked to harvest the headlands and fieldborders at a certain date and leave the field remnant for a later stage.<br/><br/><b>DISCUSSION</b><br/>CAN AN ACTIVITY FIELD EXIST OVER MORE GROWING SEASONS?<br/>WHEN SHOULD IT BE STARTED OR FINISHED?<br/>
