<b>Definition.</b><br/>A combination of man and farm machinery which can be combined and operate as one independently controllable unit to perform a <b>Task</b> which belongs to a <b>TaskCategory</b>. <br/><br/><b>Example: </b><br/>A ManMachineSystem is a driver, a tractor and a connected implement, or it can also be a driver, a tractor with two implements connected, a driver and a single self-propelled machine, an autonomous tractor without driver and an implement or a worker doing manual work.<br/>
