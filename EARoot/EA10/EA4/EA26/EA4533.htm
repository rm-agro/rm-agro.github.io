The base type for coverages is gml:AbstractCoverageType. The basic elements of a coverage can be seen in this content model: the coverage contains gml:domainSet and gml:rangeSet properties. The gml:domainSet property describes the domain of the coverage and the gml:rangeSet property describes the range of the coverage.<br/>
