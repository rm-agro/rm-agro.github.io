<b>Description.</b><br/>A <b>CulturalPractice</b> describes one or series of activities to realize an objective in crop production. Examples are primary soil tillage, planting, harvesting, etc.<br/><br/><b>Remark.</b><br/>The <b>CulturalPractice</b> does not describe the means by which the activities are performed. The means is described by <b>OperationTechnique</b>. For example primary soil tillage can be done with chisel plowing or with moldboard ploughing. Harvesting will include the harvesting process itself, but also the required transport.<br/>
