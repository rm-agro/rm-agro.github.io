<b>Description.</b><br/>A <b>DataSet</b> is a collection of <b>PropertyValue</b> 's from one<b> PropertyVariable</b> or a <b>ProductFieldApplication</b> of one product, from one object ( <b>Batch, Plot</b>) and either collected by an <b>Operation </b>(which can be a measuring Operation), or originating from a <b>DataProcess</b>.<br/><br/><b>Remark.</b><br/>An Advice is also seen as a DataProcess.<br/><br/>DataSet has as subclass SpatialDataSet, which is a DataSet that has spatial properties. As such, DataSets of Plots will generally be SpatialDataSets.<br/><br/>PropertyValue's and actual ProductFieldApplication's can be collected during a Task. In that case there are more Propertyvariables and eventualy more (Batches of) Products. A Coverage can be used for that. In a later stage this will be processed to DataSet's.<br/>
