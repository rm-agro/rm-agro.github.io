<b>Definition.</b><br/>A <b>TaskCategory</b> defines the kind of <b>Task</b>'s which can be performed by the available <b>Equipmen</b>t and that are relevant for the <b>Farm</b> or <b>Contractor</b>.<br/><br/><b>Remark.</b><br/>There are no coding lists foreseen for TaskCategory, as they are very Farm or Contractor specific.<br/><br/>On larger farms and contractor enterprises it is possible that a TaskCategory can be realised by different combinations of man and machinery.<br/><br/><b>Examples.</b><br/>Combined harrowing and drilling.<br/>
