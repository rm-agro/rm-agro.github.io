<b>Description.</b><br/><b>ImplementReferenceLine</b> is the line which forms the first path (to be) traveled by the outmost side of an<b> Implement</b> along an <b>ActivityField</b> border.<br/><br/><b>Remark.</b><br/>The<b> ImplementReferenceLine</b> is described by a <b>LineString</b>, so it can have multiple positions which are connected with a straight line. <br/><br/>It is the responsibility of the composer of the reference line 's to take care that positions are chosen on such a way that it is possible for an <b>Implement</b> to follow curved lines that are described by a <b>LineString</b>.<br/>
