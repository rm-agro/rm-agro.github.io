<b>Definition.</b><br/>The surface of a <b>Plot</b>.<br/><br/><b>Remark.</b><br/><b><br/></b>The Surface can act as surface of more plots. For example, the same surface is valid for a Field, a CropField and an ActivityField when they share the same surface. (i.e. the CropField covvers the whole Field). Or the Surface is valid for two or more CropFields, when those Cropfields share the same surface in their different growing seasons.<br/><br/><b>PlotSurface</b> is a subclass of <b>Polygon</b>. <br/><br/>
