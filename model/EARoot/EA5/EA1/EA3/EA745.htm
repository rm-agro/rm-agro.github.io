<b>Definition.</b><br/>A Job is the work executed on a particular <b>ActivityField</b> (or <b>Batch</b> and other types of objects on which work can be executed) during a certain time period by one <b>ManMachineSystem</b>, or a number of ManMachineSystem’s which cooperate. <br/><br/><b>Example.</b><br/>An example is a ManMachineSystem consisting of one combine harvester working together with a number of ManMachineSystem’s who each exist of a tractor and a trailer.<br/>
