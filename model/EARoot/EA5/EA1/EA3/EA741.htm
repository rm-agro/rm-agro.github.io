<b>Definition.</b><br/>The working width of an implement which can be controlled independently.<br/><br/><b>Remark.</b><br/>This can be a physical part of the implement like for example a part of a spraying boom or a (group of) drill coulter(s), but in case of a centrifugal fertilizer spreader or a irrigation canon, it can also be a part of the working width, not covered by the distributor itself. <br/>
