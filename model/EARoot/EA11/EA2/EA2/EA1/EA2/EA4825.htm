<b>Definition.</b><br/><b>Field</b> is a continuous piece of land, bound by topographical objects like roads, ditches, stone walls, wooded banks, farm yards, etc. and by land of another owner or operator of that land. <br/><br/><b>Remark.</b><br/>A Field is assumed to be the same for many years.<br/><br/>A whole Field can be used as <b>CropField</b> or as <b>ActivityField</b>, but a Field can also be split up in more CropField's or ActivityField's.<br/>
