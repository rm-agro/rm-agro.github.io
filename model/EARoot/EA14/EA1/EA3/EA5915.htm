<b>Definition.</b><br/>GridCoordinate is a data type for holding the grid coordinates of a CV_GridPoint. (After "Schema for coverage geometry and functions"; OGC 07-011). <br/><br/><b>Remark</b> by DG: <br/>Coordinate is a sequence of integers, so it defines the sequence in the x, y and eventual z direction. The position of those GridCoordiates can be calculated from the Origin and the OffsetVectors.<br/>
