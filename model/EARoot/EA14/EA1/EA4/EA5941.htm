Notes: <b>Definition.</b><br/>The global unique identifier to identify the Plot.<br/><br/><b>Remarks.</b><br/>1) The PlotGUID of a Plot is given by the farm enterprise that has the rights to use that field, generally over a longer period as owner or long term rent contract. The PlotGUID of an ActivityField and a CropField is given by the farm enterprise that actually uses the field during that growing season. <br/><br/>2) The PlotGUID is unique over all the years. This means that an ActivityField and a CropField will be assigned a new GUID, even when it occupies exactly the same surface as the season before.<br/>3) A PlotGUID is unique worldwide. See the definition of the GlobalUniqueIdentifierType.<br/>
