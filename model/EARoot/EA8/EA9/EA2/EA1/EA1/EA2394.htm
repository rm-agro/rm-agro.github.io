A subtype of a feature.  From the abstract specification, a Coverage is a two or more dimensional metaphor for features found on or near a portion of the Earth's Surface.  Some examples of a Coverage would be a satellite image of a portion of the earth, a census tract for the city of New York and a DEM for a USGS 1x1 degree map sheet (Topic 6, OGC Abstract Specification, 1998)<br/><br/>A coverage is a subtype of a feature that has pairs of distinguished properties consisting of a geometry property and a function that is defined over the coordinate set that is defined (delineated) by the geometry (Herring, J. OGC Change Proposal 95-039r2)<br/>
